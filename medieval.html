Medieval India (550 CE - 1526 CE)

Step into the vibrant and dynamic era of Medieval India! This period witnessed the rise and fall of powerful empires, the fusion of diverse cultures, and significant developments in art, architecture, and religion. From the emergence of regional kingdoms to the establishment of the Delhi Sultanate and the flourishing of the Vijayanagara Empire, explore the fascinating journey of a subcontinent undergoing profound transformations.
