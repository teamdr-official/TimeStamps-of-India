Recommended Books on Indian History

Delve deeper into India's rich past with our curated list of recommended books. From comprehensive overviews to specialized studies and captivating biographies, these resources offer invaluable insights into the subcontinent's diverse history.

Journey Through Knowledge

Reading is one of the most enriching ways to understand history. Books allow us to explore complex narratives, delve into the lives of historical figures, and grasp the nuances of different eras. This section provides a starting point for anyone interested in exploring the vast and fascinating history of India through literature.

General Histories & Overviews

The Wonder That Was India

A. L. Basham

A classic and comprehensive overview of Indian civilization from its origins to the arrival of the Muslims.

Find Out More

The Discovery of India

Jawaharlal Nehru

Written by India's first Prime Minister, this book offers a broad view of Indian history, philosophy, and culture.

Find Out More

A History of India (Vol. 1)

Romila Thapar

A detailed academic insight into early Indian history up to 1300 AD, part of a multi-volume set.

Ancient India

Early India: From the Origins to AD 1300

Romila Thapar

A definitive work exploring early Indian history, focusing on social, economic, and cultural developments.

Gregory L. Possehl

An authoritative and updated account of the Harappan civilization, incorporating recent archaeological findings.

The Mughal Empire

Modern India & Independence Movement

A monumental work detailing India's journey from independence to the early 21st century.

A gripping narrative account of the events leading up to India's independence and partition.
